Inside Mommy, Tend Your Heart ...

The goal of Christian mothers is to raise God-fearing children who will love and serve God with all their hearts...

Becoming a mother is a life-long commitment. It requires perseverance and self-sacrifice empowered by the Holy Spirit and guided by Godâ€™s Word. If a mother tries to parent her children apart from these resources, she will find herself weary and discouraged.

Yvonne encourages mothers to find hope and joy in Jesus Christ. She challenges them to examine their hearts in light of Scripture ... Yvonne teaches mothers that joyful, biblical parenting always begins first in their hearts. Only as a mother learns to love Jesus Christ through obedience to His Word will she find strength when she is weary, comfort when she is heartbroken, joy when she is facing challenges, guidance when the path before her is unclear, and encouragement to persevere.

Additional Resources from Yvonne

Yvonne created a course for young ladies titled Heart of Virtue. In this school-year-long course, targeted to 16-year-old girls, students learned a combination of biblical application and practical skills - such as cooking, baking, cleaning, shopping, dressing-well, etiquette, and personal care.

Out of this class developed a need for a "textbook." This was the impetus for
Rare - A Young Woman Who Fears the Lord

Rare quickly became a popular book for ladies' Bible study. Consequently, a study guide of practical application of the concepts presented in Rare seemed useful. It has proven to be a profitable resource for both group and discipling studies.
